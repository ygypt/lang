# sounds and romanization
## vowels / emotions / colors
```
o : sorrow
a : bliss
u : apathy
e : rage
i : terror
```
## consonants
| noise | timbre | hit | roman |
|:---|:---|:---|:---|
| s | z | ts   | `s z q` |
| sh | jj | ch | `c j h` |
| r | rr | d   | `l r d` |
| th | thz | t | `y b t` |
| x | xr | k   | `x g k` |
| f | v | p    | `f v p` |
