## base 10 number system ;)

#### da numba chart
|place|block|-|value|block|
|---|---|---|---|---|
|0     |`t'`|-|0|`k'`|
|10    |`to`|-|1|`ko`|
|100   |`ta`|-|2|`ka`|
|1000  |`tu`|-|3|`ku`|
|10000 |`te`|-|4|`ke`|
|100000|`ti`|-|5|`ki`|

#### how to use it
(i will be refering to decimal numbers as d#)

numbers are spoken as such

```{value.place}.{value.place}.{etc..```

for instance,

1 (d1) is `t'ko` or `ko`

5 (d5) is `t'ki` or `ki`

10 (d6) is `toko`

11 (d7) is `tokot`ko`

23 (d15) is `tokat`ku`

524 (d195) is `takitokat'ke`

##### to break that last one down

`{100.5}.{10.2}.{0.4}`
+ 5 in the 100th place
  + 2 in the 10th place
    + 4 in the 0th place

`{ta.ki}.{to.ka}.{t'.ke}`\
aka\
`taki.toka.t'ke`\
aka\
`takitokat'ke`
